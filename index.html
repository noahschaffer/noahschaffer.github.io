Bio

Hi! I'm Noah!

I completed my BS/MS in Computer Science at Northwestern University, where I had the pleasure of working in the Interactive Audio Lab under the supervision of Prof. Bryan Pardo and Dr. Ethan Manilow. My research focused on audio enhancement and source separation.

My research interests lie in the intersection between Music Information Retrieval and Audio Generation. I am interested in leveraging generative models in audio to improve music information retrieval tasks such as source separation. I am also interested in improving techniques in audio generation, particularly for music.

I currently live in Chicago, where I spend my free time playing guitar, listening to copious amounts of rock, walking by Lake Michigan, and watching New York sports.
